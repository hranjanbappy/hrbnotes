# Composite Beam

A steel-concrete composite beam used as the validation benchmark for
[[SAFIR]] thermal-structural runs and referenced by [[Fire Design]].

## Properties

| Property        | Value   |
|-----------------|---------|
| Span            | 6.0 m   |
| Steel section   | IPE 300 |
| Concrete slab   | 120 mm  |

The mid-span deflection under the standard fire curve is the key acceptance
metric. #fire #benchmark
