# Fire Design

Structural fire design ensures load-bearing members keep enough capacity during
a fire for the required resistance period.

## Methods

- **Prescriptive** — tabulated data, simple but conservative.
- **Performance-based** — uses tools like [[SAFIR]] for realistic behaviour.

A typical [[Composite Beam]] check combines the heating curve with reduced
material strengths.
